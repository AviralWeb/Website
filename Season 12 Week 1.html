PUBG Season 12 - Tips and Tricks to complete Royal Pass Missons

Since,pubg is very successful on mobile alot players like to play it on thier PC's.Time has passed and now PUBG Season 12 is here that means new weapon skins,new emotes,new dresses and new missions and what not.Here i am going to tell you how to complete all season 12 week missions so that you can reach to RP 100 really easily.

So lets get started...

Week 1

Mission 1-For 150 RP points

  • Deal a total of 3600 damage in Classic mode
  • Deal 7500 damage in Arena
  • Deal 4000 damage in Payload

Well,you just need to complete only one mission from others are alternative.Just play any match in Arena, there are endless respawns so you can do it easily there.Play Domination specificly to complete this real quick.

Mission 2-For 75 RP points

  • Travel for a total of 20000 meters while driving vehicles a(n) in Classic mode

Drive 20000 meters though any vehicle and this mission will be completed.You can complete this in a single match also just land,find a vehicle and drive it till the end of match and you are done.(Dont worry about fuel you will find another vehicles on your way.)

Mission 3-For 75 RP points

  • Pick up smoke granades over 20 matches in Classic mode

Pick up smoke granades in 20 different matches and this one is done.

Mission 4-For 75 RP points

  • Kill an enemy from 200 meter away 1 time in Classic mode

For this i have an amazing trick,enter a duo or a squad match knock an enemy or bot(don't kill him,only knock him ) and run 200 meters away from him and when he dies your mission will be completed.

Mission 5-For 100 RP points

  • Land on any rooftop in Pochinki(Erangel) 3 times in Classic mode
  • Land on any rooftop in Los Leones(Miramar) 3 times in Classic mode
  • Land on any rooftop in Quarry(Sanhok) 3 times in Classic mode

Land on rooftop of any of these places 3 places 3 times and you are done.Remember only one mission is to be done others are alternative.

Mission 6-For 50 RP points

  • Kill 2 enemies while driving a vehicle in Classic Mode

Kill 2 enemies from your vehicle when you drive.For this just drop in the end of map with other offline players take vehicle and crush them out.

Mission 7-For 50 RP points

  • Kill 7 enemies with shotgun in Erangel in Classic mode

You need to kill 7 enemies from shotgun that is S12k,S686 and S1897.Choose KJRP server 1 VS 4 and kill them all.

Mission 8-For 50 RP points

  • Finish in the Top 3 with friends 2 times in Classic mode

To complete this you need to play with friends so enter squad match and try to survive till the end.Try to have safe landing dont paly rush game because there is a chance you will die.(Remember even if all members die except one this mission can be completed for everyone,the last remaining one needs too survive in top 3)

Mission 9-For 50 RP points

  • Revive teammates 5 times in Classic mode

Revive teammates 5 times,this mission will be easy in squad match or if you have your friend just tell him to knock himself 5 times so that you can revive him.

Mission 10-For 50 RP points

  • Complete 14 matches in Arena

Play any arena match(domination,TDM,Assault,Arena training) 14 times.
